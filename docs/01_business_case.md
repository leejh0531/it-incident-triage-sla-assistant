# Business Case

## 1. Client Situation

The client manages internal IT incidents through email and spreadsheets.  
As the number of tickets increases, it becomes difficult to identify high-priority issues, monitor SLA risk, and reuse existing troubleshooting knowledge.

## 2. Business Problem

- Ticket priority is judged manually.
- SLA violation risk is not visible before the deadline.
- Similar IT issues are repeatedly reported.
- Managers cannot quickly identify operational bottlenecks.
- Tickets may be assigned to inappropriate teams.

## 3. Goal

Build a simple IT operations support system that helps triage tickets, monitor SLA risk, recommend responsible teams, and search related FAQs.

## 4. Expected Value

- Faster initial ticket triage
- Better visibility of SLA risk
- Reduced manual decision-making
- Improved reuse of FAQ knowledge
- Better operational insight for IT support teams
